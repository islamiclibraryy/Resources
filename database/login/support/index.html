Trouble Logging In?

If you are unable to access your account, please do not panic. Simply ensure the following points to successfully log in:

  • Google Login (New ✨): The fastest way! If you connected your Google account, simply click the "Continue with Google" button on the login page to enter without a password.
  • Check your Credentials: Ensure you are entering the exact Mobile Number or Email Address you used during registration.
  • No Extra Spaces: Make sure there are no blank spaces before or after your email or password. This is a very common mistake.
  • Correct Input Mode: Use the "Email" option for email IDs, and the "Mobile" option if you registered using a phone number. Ensure the correct option is highlighted blue.

Still Need Help?

If you're still unable to login after checking the above steps, send us a direct message on Instagram. We will check our database and help you regain access.

Creating an Account

Joining the Islamic Library is completely free and takes only a few seconds. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Google Signup (New ✨): Skip filling out forms! Just tap the "Continue with Google" button to instantly create a secure account using your existing Google profile.
  • Choose a Method: You can sign up using either a valid Email Address or your Mobile Number. We recommend using an email for better security and easy password resets.
  • Strong Password: Create a password that you can easily remember but is hard for others to guess.
  • Account Exists? If you see an "Account already in use" error, it means you have already registered. Try logging in or resetting your password instead of signing up again.

Need Assistance?

Facing errors while creating a new account? Take a screenshot of the error and share it with us on Instagram.

Resetting Your Password

Forgot your password? Don't worry, here is how you can recover your account based on how you registered:

If you use Google Login:

No Password Needed: You don't have a library password! Just go back to the login page and click "Continue with Google". If you forgot your Google password, please reset it directly through Google.

If you registered with an Email:

Simply enter your email on the Reset Password page. A secure link will be sent to your inbox.
Note: If you don't see the email, please check your Spam or Junk folder.

If you registered with a Mobile Number:

Action Required: Because mobile accounts are not linked to an email address, the system cannot automatically send you a reset link. You will need to contact our support team to manually reset your password.

Manual Password Reset

If you used a Mobile Number, or if the email reset link isn't working, message us your registered details and we'll reset it for you.
