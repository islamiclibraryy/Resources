Welcome to Islamic Library

Assalamu Alaikum. Welcome to Islamic Library by Shekh, a dedicated digital platform designed to make authentic Islamic knowledge easily accessible to the Ummah.

Our mission is to spread the light of Hikmah (wisdom) by providing a vast collection of Islamic eBooks, Quranic PDFs, Hadith collections, and historical literature directly to your devices.

Free & Premium Content

We firmly believe that Islamic knowledge should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial status. Therefore, the majority of the books and resources in our library are absolutely 100% FREE for anyone to download and read.

However, to support the maintenance of this platform, cover server costs, and continue adding high-quality content, we may occasionally feature exclusive Premium/Paid collections. Purchasing these premium books directly supports the library and helps us keep the free resources available forever.

Jazakallah Khair for being a part of our journey.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is highly respected and strictly protected at Islamic Library by Shekh.

  • Information We Collect: We only collect basic details such as your Name, Mobile Number (used as your unique login ID), and Password.
  • Payment Data: We do NOT collect or store your bank account details, UPI PINs, or credit card information. Payments are processed securely via external UPI apps, and we only ask for the UTR/Transaction ID to verify your purchase.
  • Data Security: Your personal library, downloaded books, and account details are securely stored. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third-party marketing agencies.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to keep you logged in and to cache data locally on your device. This makes the app run faster and saves your internet data.
