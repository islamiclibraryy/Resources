About The Holy Quran

The Holy Quran is the final and complete revelation from Allah (SWT) to humanity, revealed to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) over 23 years. Preserved in its original Arabic form, it serves as a complete guide for faith, morality, and daily life. The Quran addresses spiritual, ethical, social, and legal aspects of life, providing guidance for individuals and communities. This edition includes the original Arabic text, Urdu translation in Roman script, and easy Arabic transliteration, making it accessible for those who cannot read Arabic or Urdu. It is designed for reading, understanding, and reflecting upon the divine message. Perfect for truth seekers and those who wish to explore the teachings of Islam in a simple and accessible format.
